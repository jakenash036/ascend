EDITING STYLE

CORE FEELING

Controlled intensity.

The editing should feel:

* cinematic
* ambitious
* premium
* powerful
* modern
* controlled
* slightly mysterious

NOT:

* hyperactive
* childish
* meme-heavy
* fake billionaire
* cringe motivation edits

The vibe is:
motion + control + ambition.

---

PACING

Fast but deliberate.

Use:

* smooth cuts
* rhythmic pacing
* tension build-up
* controlled speed

NOT:

* chaotic editing
* random cuts
* overstimulation

---

CLIP STYLE

Use:

* night driving
* city lights
* reflections
* watches
* movement
* keyboards
* laptops
* gyms
* coffee shops
* elevators
* parking garages
* money flashes
* parties
* walking shots
* POV shots

The footage should feel:
constant motion and momentum.

---

CAMERA STYLE

Best angles:

* closeups
* low angles
* POV
* silhouette shots
* reflection shots
* moving shots

---

LIGHTING STYLE

Dark.
High contrast.
Low saturation.

Use:

* shadows
* highlights
* reflections
* cinematic lighting

---

COLOUR GRADING

Main look:

* slightly desaturated
* cold blacks
* silver highlights
* crushed shadows
* high contrast

Avoid:

* oversaturated colours
* bright influencer orange tones
* rainbow edits
* flashy gold overlays

---

TEXT STYLE

Fonts:

* Montserrat Bold
* Helvetica Neue Bold

Style:

* uppercase
* minimal
* strong statements
* clean spacing

Examples:

* ESCAPE AVERAGE
* BUILD LEVERAGE
* CONTROL YOUR LIFE
* OWN THE SYSTEM

Avoid:

* clickbait text
* emoji spam
* exaggerated money claims

---

SUBTITLES

Style:

* clean
* white text
* subtle black shadow/background
* minimal animation

Avoid:

* rainbow captions
* bouncing subtitles
* excessive effects

---

MUSIC STYLE

Use:

* cinematic trap
* phonk
* dark ambient
* tension music
* emotional instrumentals
* deep bass

Avoid:

* meme sounds
* comedy audios
* generic TikTok music

---

TRANSITIONS

Use:

* motion blur
* speed ramps
* quick fades
* subtle transitions

Avoid:

* overediting
* spinning effects
* crazy transitions
* excessive zooms

---

SOUND DESIGN

Use subtle:

* car sounds
* footsteps
* keyboard clicks
* city ambience
* bass hits
* transition sounds

Goal:
create immersion and intensity.

---

THE GOLDEN RULE

Understatement creates aura.

Everything should feel:

* controlled
* deliberate
* confident
* premium
* cinematic
* modern
